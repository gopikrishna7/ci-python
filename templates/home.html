About Us

We are a South Indian breakfast restaurant, serving delicious traditional dishes such as dosa, idli, vada and more. Our ingredients are fresh and sourced locally, and our chefs have years of experience preparing authentic South Indian cuisine. Come visit us and taste the difference!

We believe in using only the finest ingredients, and our dishes are made with care and attention to detail. Our goal is to provide our customers with a truly authentic South Indian breakfast experience, and to bring a little piece of the south to your plate.

Whether you're a fan of spicy and savory, or sweet and tangy, we have something for everyone. Our menu is designed to cater to a wide range of tastes, and we are always experimenting with new and innovative recipes to keep things fresh and exciting.

Daily Specials

In addition to our regular menu, we offer a rotating selection of daily specials. From new twists on classic dishes to seasonal ingredients and flavors, there's always something new and exciting to try at our restaurant. Come visit us and see what we're cooking up today!
